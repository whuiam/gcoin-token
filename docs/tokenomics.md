# GCOIN Tokenomics (v1.0)

## Total Supply
**66,000,000 GCOIN**

No additional minting is possible.  
Supply is fixed permanently.

---

## Distribution Model
- **Public Market:** 100%  
- **Team Allocation:** 0%  
- **Marketing:** 0%  
- **Presale:** 0%  
- **Developer Reserve:** 0%

GCOIN is a fair-token model with no hidden allocations.

---

## Decimals
18

---

## Liquidity Policy
GCOIN uses PancakeSwap V2 as its primary trading liquidity.

Liquidity actions follow these rules:
- No manual LP removal without community notice  
- Future LP locks planned  
- No team-controlled tax system  

---

## Trading Fees
GCOIN has **no built-in trading fees** in the contract.  
All fees are handled by the exchange (e.g., PancakeSwap).

---

GCOIN follows a clean, transparent tokenomics structure with no hidden mechanics, no taxes, and no unfair distribution.
